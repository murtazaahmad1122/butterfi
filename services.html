BF Matching

Our self-assessment tool to prepare your full funding profile and unlock your financing potential. You will be asked about 20 questions (average completion time – 9:25 minutes) and you will get a full report to better understand and prepare your next funding round , including a comprehansive analysis on the most relevant financing models for your company. Butterfi is now ready to introduce you to those funders who seek companies with an exact same profile like yours. Butterfly funding process is faster and generates more funding offers, higher success rate for closing and in better funding terms for you.

BF Analytics

BF Analytics outlines the strengths and compatibility of a specific funding offer which your received, including benchmarking to market standards and offers comparison. BF Analytics tool does not only calculate the overall cost of capital of each offer (APR Calculator), but it can also analyze and compare other key elements in an offer such as negative/financial covenants, early repayment terms and equity kickers. BF Analytics provides insights, reports and visualization which you can share with your management and BOD throughout the process.

BF Monitoring

Post-transaction monitoring and reassessment tool BF Monitoring includes, Assessment of business results and compliance with the deal’s covenants, dynamic evaluation of a cost of capital, signals on re-finance opportunities, Insights on currency exposures and hedging requirements.

BF Reporting
